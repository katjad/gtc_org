They are still in their beginning stages, the latest addition being the Knowledge Commons: the [blog](https://growingcommons.substack.com/) for connecting with kindred spirits, the [forum](https://forum.growingthecommons.org/) for discussing, and the [wiki](https://knowledge.growingthecommons.org/) for curating knowledge (with no strict separation of course, all of them are for connecting people).
